About IEECIT

IEECIT 2026 brings together leading academics, researchers, and industry experts from around the globe to share cutting-edge innovations and advancements in the fields of Electrical, Electronic, Computer, and Information Technology. The conference serves as a premier platform for knowledge exchange, collaboration, and the presentation of pioneering research that shapes the future of technology and engineering. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their work, exchange ideas, and explore collaborative opportunities with peers worldwide. Together, we aim to inspire innovation, strengthen academic-industry partnerships, and contribute to sustainable technological growth.

Track 01: Artificial Intelligence, Data & Computing

  • Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, and Machine Learning
  • Data Mining, Data Analytics, and Big Data
  • Pattern Recognition, Prediction, and Tracking
  • Quantum Computing
  • Neuromorphic Computing
  • E-Commerce and E-Governance

  • Computer Architecture and Systems
  • Computer Networks and Security
  • Distributed, Cloud, Edge, and Fog Computing
  • Internet and Web Applications
  • Software Engineering and Database Systems

Track 03: Electronics, Devices & Circuits
  • VLSI Circuits and Systems
  • Nanotechnology, MEMS, and Emerging Devices
  • Electronic and Photonic Materials
